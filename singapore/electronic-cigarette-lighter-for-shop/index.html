What are Electronic Cigarette Lighters?

Electronic cigarette lighters are devices designed to ignite electronic cigarettes, also known as e-cigarettes or vaporizers. They use a heating element, usually a coil or a ceramic element, to vaporize the e-liquid, which is then inhaled by the user. These lighters are a safer and more convenient alternative to traditional flame lighters, which can be hazardous and unreliable. In Singapore, where the smoking ban is strictly enforced, electronic cigarette lighters have become a popular choice among smokers who are looking for a safer and more discreet way to enjoy their e-cigarettes.

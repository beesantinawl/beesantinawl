Understanding Electronic Cigarettes

Electronic cigarettes, also known as e-cigarettes, are devices that simulate the experience of smoking by producing a vapor that resembles smoke. They typically contain a battery, a heating element, and a liquid solution that may or may not contain nicotine. The liquid solution is often flavored, and the vapor produced can have a variety of flavors. E-cigarettes have been marketed as a safer alternative to traditional tobacco products, but their health effects are still being studied and debated.

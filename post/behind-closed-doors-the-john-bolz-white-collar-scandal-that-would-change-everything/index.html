Before we dive into the John Bolz case, it’s essential to understand what white-collar crime is. White-collar crime refers to non-violent, financially motivated crimes committed by individuals or organizations for personal or financial gain. White-collar crimes are often committed by individuals in positions of power or authority, making them difficult to detect and prosecute.

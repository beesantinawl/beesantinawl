Introduction to the Nifty 50 Index

The Nifty 50 index, also known as the India 50, is a stock market index that represents the 50 largest and most liquid companies listed on the National Stock Exchange of India. Understanding the Nifty 50 Index

The Nifty 50 index is calculated and maintained by the NSE Indices, a subsidiary of the National Stock Exchange of India. It is one of the two main stock indices used in India, the other being the BSE Sensex. The Nifty 50 index is widely used by investors, financial institutions, and researchers as a benchmark to measure the performance of the Indian stock market.
